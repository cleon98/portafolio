Who I Am?

I am an undergraduated professional in International Relations. After my graduation I was determined to enroll in a graduate program and I did it. I was admitted to a master about International Cooperation for the Development, sadly some situations out of my control denied me the opportunity to go ahead with my plan.

After not being able to start my studies I was not sure about what to do next so in the aftermath I decided to start learning about programming. I always had a genuine interest but never actually took the time to learn. I got summerged in the topics and felt so passionate about it but I had a problem, there were moments when I would not understand something and would stay stuck there.

With this passion just discovered I decided to get into a Full-Stack web developer bootcamp and after some really hard and intense months I got to finish it adquiring the skills demanded by the market to be a high value professional.
